# Dashboard Story

## Executive Summary

The Executive Sales Performance Dashboard provides management with a comprehensive overview of business performance by combining sales trends, regional performance, product profitability, discount analysis, shipping efficiency, and customer behavior into one interactive dashboard.

The company generated approximately $217.02M in total sales, $33.31M in total profit, and maintained a profit margin of 15.35%.

## What is Performing Well

The South region leads both sales and profit, making it the strongest regional market.

Technology products consistently generate the highest profits across all product categories.

Monthly sales remain stable overall despite seasonal fluctuations.

## What is Underperforming

Furniture experiences the highest number of returned orders.

Standard Class shipping has the longest delivery time.

Higher discounts are associated with declining profits.

## Risks

Increasing discounts may continue reducing profitability.

Long delivery times could negatively impact customer satisfaction.

High furniture returns increase operational costs.

## Opportunities

Expand Technology product offerings.

Adopt successful South region sales strategies in other regions.

Improve shipping efficiency to enhance customer experience.

Optimize discount strategies to protect profit margins.

## Recommended Business Actions

- Strengthen Technology product marketing.
- Improve Furniture quality and return management.
- Reduce unnecessary discounting.
- Improve Standard Class delivery performance.
- Expand successful regional sales practices.

## Limitations

The dashboard uses historical sales data and does not include forecasting, customer feedback, or competitor analysis.

## Suggested Next Analysis

Future analysis should include customer lifetime value, product return reasons, sales forecasting, and customer retention analysis.
